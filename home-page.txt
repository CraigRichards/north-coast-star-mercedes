Mercedes Benz Servicing & Star Diagnostics Gold Coast
Are you looking for exceptional mechanics to service or repair your Mercedes Benz with the care and precision your car was originally built with?
Welcome to North Coast Star, formerly James For Mercedes. My name is James Mulholland and my business partner is Geoff Hughes. Between us we have over 60 years experience working on Mercedes Benz vehicles.
We are not only experienced Mercedes Benz Mechanics, but Independent Factory Trained Mercedes Benz Technicians with over 25 years experience each. We are able to work on all the latest Mercedes Benz models using all the updated technology to service and repair your car.
We have a workshop on the northern end of the Gold Coast in Helensvale called North Coast Star. As  specialist Mercedes Benz mechanics and technicians on the Gold Coast, we can service and repair your car without the expensive price tags of the dealerships.
We work exclusively on all models of Mercedes Benz and know the nuances and intricacies of each one and perform full factory standard diagnostics to have your car running to the highest Mercedes standards.
We can offer independent advice when you are buying a used Mercedes and can have your car ready to pass a Roadworthy Certificate.
We look forward to giving your car great servicing and saving you money.
